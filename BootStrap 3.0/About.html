The Orgin

GamerStore traces its roots to Babbage's, a Dallas, Texas-based software retailer founded in 1984 by former Harvard Business School classmates James McCurry and Gary M. Kusin.

What started off as a small platform for Valve to provide updates to its games has turned into the largest computer gaming platform in the world. In September 2021, Steam reached a peak of over 26.09 million concurrent users. The platform's record was from May 2021, peaking at 26.85 million concurrent users.
